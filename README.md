<h1 align="center">ChatTracker</h1> <p align="center"> <img src="https://github.com/Mikeym41/Chattracker/blob/master/docs/Screenshot%202025-09-01%20223401.png?raw=true" alt="ChatTracker Logo" width="200"> </p> <p align="center"> Track conversations, analyze chat logs, and keep your server community safe and fun. </p> <hr> <h2>Description</h2> <p> <strong>ChatTracker</strong> monitors all chat activity on your server, logging messages with timestamps and player names so you always have a record. Its <strong>Big Brother</strong> feature checks messages for banned words or phrases, flags them, and notifies online moderators automatically.

Fully implemented now: <strong>Staff Chat</strong> lets moderators communicate privately without interfering with public chat.
All flagged messages are logged separately, and banned words are fully configurable in <code>config.yml</code>.

Additionally, <strong>hoverable and clickable reports</strong> allow players to report inappropriate messages in-game directly to staff.

ChatTracker helps staff stay on top of rule-breaking behavior efficiently and reliably, with enhanced message formatting to make chat clear and readable.
